Philodryas arnaldoi (AMARAL, 1932)

Higher TaxaColubridae (Dipsadinae), Colubroidea, Caenophidia, Alethinophidia, Serpentes, Squamata (snakes)
Subspecies 
Common NamesE: Arnaldo's Green Racer
Portuguese: Papa-Pinto, Papa-Rato, Parelheira-Clara, Parelheira-do-Mato 
SynonymChlorosoma arnaldoi AMARAL 1932: 100
Philodryas arnaldoi — GRAZZIOTIN et al. 2012
Philodryas arnaldoi — WALLACH et al. 2014: 549
Philodryas arnaldoi — NOGUEIRA et al. 2019
Philodryas arnaldoi — ARREDONDO et al. 2020
Pseudablabes arnaldoi — MELO-SAMPAIO et al. 2020 
DistributionBrazil (Sao Paulo, Parana, Rio Grande do Sul, Santa Catarina)

Type locality: S. Bento, Santa Catarina, Brazil.  
Reproductionoviparous 
TypesHolotype: IBSP (= IB) 7807, female 
Diagnosis 
CommentHabitat: fully arboreal (Harrington et al. 2018).

Phylogenetics: MELO-SAMPAIO et al. 2020 tentatively included arnaldoi within Pseudablabes (based on morphology) although they did not have any molecular data. Note that Arredondo et al. 2020 could not replicate the monophyly of Pseudablabes as defined by Melo-Sampaio et al., hence we reverted them to Philodryas. 
EtymologyNamed after Arnaldo França, Amaral's technical assistant.
