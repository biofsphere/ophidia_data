Type locality: “Indiis”

brachyurus: ES,SE Brazil; Type locality: Brazil, Matto Grosso, Chapada.

forsteri: E Brazil  
Reproductionoviparous 
TypesHolotype: NRM (also given as RMS)
Syntypes: ANSP 11202-03 [brachyurus]
Holotype: CM 32, collected by J. Steinbach in February 1913 [elaeoides] 
Diagnosis 
CommentSynonymy after PETERS & OREJAS-MIRANDA 1970.

Distribution: Not in Argentina according to the map in LEYNAUD & BUCHER 1999. See map in Nogueira et al. 2019.

Subspecies: No subspecies fide DIXON 1980 but 2 fide DIXON 1987.

Scalation: Linne's specimen had 137 ventrals and 70 caudals. Counts from Colombia are: 135-153 ventrals (av. of 14 is 142.8);
57-76 male caudals (av. of 5 is 69.6); 55-68 female caudals (av. of 6 is 59.3) (from Dunn 1944: 482)
