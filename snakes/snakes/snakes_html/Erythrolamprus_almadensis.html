DistributionBrazil (Rio Grande do Sul, Maranhão [HR 30: 113], Rondonia, Pará, Minas Gerais, Rio de Janeiro), Paraguay, Argentina (Chaco, Corrientes, Formosa), Uruguay, Bolivia, Peru

Type locality: Almada, Bahia, Brazil.  
Reproductionoviparous 
TypesLectotype: ZSM 2688/0, juvenile, desginated by HOOGMOED & GRUBER 1983 
Diagnosis 
CommentSynonymy after CEI 1993, Dixon 1989.

Distribution: This species has been reported from Peru, but without specific locality (T. Doan, pers. comm. 30 Apr 2012). See map in Nogueira et al. 2019.

Type species: Natrix almada WAGLER 1824: 30 is the type species of the genus Leimadophis Fitzinger 1843. See Dunn 1944 for a definition of the genus. 
EtymologyNamed after the type locality.
