Higher TaxaColubridae (Dipsadinae), Colubroidea, Caenophidia, Alethinophidia, Serpentes, Squamata (snakes)
Subspecies 
Common NamesE: Jan's Hognose Snake
Portuguese: Cobra-Coral, Falsa-Coral, Nariguda-Rajada 
SynonymHeterodon histricus JAN 1863: 224
Heterodon histricus — BOULENGER 1886: 434
Lystrophis histricus — BOULENGER 1894
Lystrophis histricus — CEI 1993
Lystrophis histricus — GIRAUDO & SCROCCHI 2002
Xenodon histricus — ZAHER et al. 2009
Xenodon histricus — PRIGIONI et al. 2011
Lystrophis histricus — WALLACH et al. 2014: 406
Xenodon histricus — NOGUEIRA et al. 2019 
DistributionS Brazil (Rio Grande do Sul, Paraná), Uruguay, Paraguay (border to Minas Gerais), NE Argentina (San Luis, Chaco, Corrientes, Formosa, La Pampa, and Misiones)

Type locality: South America fide Jan and Sordelli (1865).  
Reproductionoviparous 
TypesHolotype: lost, formerly MSNM (Milano), 350 mm, destroyed in 1943 during World War II. 
Diagnosis 
CommentSynonymy partly after CEI 1993. HOGE et al. (1975) removed L. nattereri from the synonym of L. histricus. However, subsequent authors still considered it as synonym of L. histricus.
